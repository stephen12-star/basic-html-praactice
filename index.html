Sleeping

Disadvanteges

It is required for the body to get seven hours of sleep to funtion properely.

There are steps to sleeping and dreaming. To get a goods night rest you should not look at cellular or computer devices or screens within 30 minutes. Within 90 minutes of being asleep you will enter REM sleep stage which will cause dreams. Then there's lucid dreaming where you can control your dreams and cause anything to happen. This stage can help tackle problems by playing the scenario in your dream which may feel real. The benefits of lucid dreaming include:

  • Less Anxiety
  • Improved problem-solving
There are consequences however like
  • Confusion
  • Delirium
  • Sleep paralysis

This being said there are drawbacks to everything in life and it should be nothing to worry about! Make sure to get good quality sleep and Dream On!
